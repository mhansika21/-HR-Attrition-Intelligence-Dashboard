HR Attrition Intelligence Dashboard



🏢 Project Overview
This project delivers an interactive HR Analytics Dashboard built to identify and visualize employee attrition patterns. By analyzing a dataset of 10,000 employees, the dashboard pinpoints high-risk segments based on demographics, compensation, and workload. 
Business Goal: Empower HR teams to reduce turnover costs (estimated at 1.5x–2x an employee's annual salary) through data-driven retention strategies.

🎯 Objectives
Quantify Risk: Calculate real-time attrition rates across different departments and roles.
Identify Drivers: Isolate key reasons for departure (e.g., Overtime, low Salary, or lack of Tenure).
Interactive Filtering: Enable HR managers to drill down by Department, Gender, and Work-Life balance factors.
Decision Support: Provide automated insights to guide management in policy adjustments.

📊 Dashboard Features
📌 Live KPI Cards: Instant visibility into Total Headcount, Attrition Rate (%), Avg. Salary, and Avg. Tenure.
⏳ Overtime Burnout Analysis: Visualizes the strong correlation between excessive hours and resignation.
💰 Salary-Retention Matrix: Box plots identifying pay gaps between stayers and leavers.
📍 Department Risk Heatmap: Quickly identifies departments like Sales or Tech with above-average turnover.
📂 Data Export: Ability to download filtered "at-risk" employee lists for HR follow-ups.
